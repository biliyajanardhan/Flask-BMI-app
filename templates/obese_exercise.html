Here are some Exercises.

Walking in Place (Cardio)

Simply walk in place, lifting your knees higher for added intensity. Use your arms to increase the workout.

Time: Start with 5-10 minutes and gradually increase to 30 minutes.

Play Video
Chair Squats (Lower Body):

Stand in front of a sturdy chair with feet shoulder-width apart. Slowly lower your body toward the chair as if you were going to sit down, then stand back up.

Time: Start with 1 set of 10 repetitions and gradually increase to 3 sets of 15 repetitions.

Play Video
Wall Push-Ups (Upper Body)

Stand facing a wall at arm's length. Place your hands on the wall at shoulder height. Bend your elbows and lean in toward the wall, then push back out to the starting position.

Time: Start with 1 set of 5 repetitions and gradually increase to 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

Play Video
Seated Leg Lifts (Core and Legs)

Sit on the edge of a sturdy chair with your feet flat on the floor. Lift one leg straight out in front of you, then lower it back down. Alternate legs.

Time: Start with 1 set of 10 repetitions per leg and gradually increase to 3 sets of 15 repetitions per leg.

Play Video
Standing Side Leg Lifts (Legs and Hips)

Stand next to a sturdy surface for support if needed. Lift one leg out to the side, then lower it back down. Alternate legs.

Time: Start with 1 set of 10 repetitions per leg and gradually increase to 3 sets of 15 repetitions per leg.

Play Video
Marching in Place with Arm Raises (Cardio and Upper Body)

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and arms by your sides. Lift your knees up as high as comfortable while raising your arms overhead. Lower arms and repeat

Time: Start with 5 minutes and gradually increase to 15-20 minutes.
