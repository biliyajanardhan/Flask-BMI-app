Here are some Exercises.

Walking

Aim for at least 30 minutes of brisk walking most days of the week.

You can split this into smaller sessions if needed, such as three 10-minute walks throughout the day

Cycling

Start with 20-30 minutes of cycling at a comfortable pace

Three to four times per week, and gradually increase both the duration and intensity as you build stamina.

Play Video
Chair Exercises

Engage in chair exercises for about 20-30 minutes, three to four times per week.

You can incorporate these exercises into your daily routine, such as during breaks at work or while watching TV.

Play Video
Strength Training

Perform strength training exercises with resistance bands or light weights for about 20-30 minutes, two to three times per week

Allow at least one day of rest between strength training sessions to allow muscles to recover.

Play Video
Jumping Jacks

Stand with feet together, arms at your sides.Jump while spreading your legs shoulder-width apart and raising your arms overhead.

Aim for 3 sets of 30-60 seconds.

Play Video
Wall Sits

Lean against a wall with your feet shoulder-width apart and slide down until your knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.

Hold this position for as long as possible, aiming for 30-60 seconds.Aim for 3 sets.
